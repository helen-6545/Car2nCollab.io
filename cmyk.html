Digital artwork can be made in RGB and in CMYK. CMYK is designed to stay true to color when printed. Most notably, it dulls overly saturated colors that a printer cannot replicate. Since everything for this club is being made with the intention to print, do a favor to your future self and work in CMYK from the beginning, especially if you use bright colors.

(Traditional artists disregard)

2. the same artwork automatically changed to CMYK
